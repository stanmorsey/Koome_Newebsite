Who We Are

Operating from our headquarters at Wilson Airport in Nairobi, Kenya

Skyjet Supplies Services Limited was founded in 2022 by seasoned aircraft maintenance engineers, Clarence Onzere Mulusa and Bethel Koome Murega. With a deep commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, Skyjet Supplies began as a modest mobile shop offering essential aircraft maintenance components to local aviation facilities and small regional airlines.

We are committed to staying up-to-date with latest and ealierliest aircraft parts and providing exceptional customer service to ensure our customers look and feel their best.
